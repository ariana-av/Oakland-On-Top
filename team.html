Ariana Astorga Vega

Ariana is a 17-year-old entering her last year of high school with a profound passion for coding. In addition to her technical pursuits, Ariana has a deep sense of pride in her heritage and hometown. She created this website as a tribute to her roots, celebrating the vibrant culture and community of Oakland where she was born and raised. Through her work, Ariana aims to inspire others and showcase the unique spirit of her beloved city.
