What Is Vision Quilt?
Vision Quilt is a volunteer grassroots organization based in Portland, Oregon. Our members are teachers, business owners, parents, artists, social workers, journalists, retirees and gun owners. In fact, our first panels were created by gun owners. We bring years of experience in working with public schools, universities, nonprofits and social justice causes.
